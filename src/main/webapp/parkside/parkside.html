Parkside Kitchen and Grill

A Little About Parkside

Parkside Restaurant & Grill offers a unique dining experience with a focus on fresh, made-to-order meals and international cuisine. The venue features multiple cooking stations, including a grill, wok station, and extensive salad bar.

Located in the Parkside International Residential College, this dining facility serves as a hub for cultural exchange through food, offering authentic dishes from around the world while accommodating various dietary preferences.

Regular cultural celebrations and themed events showcase international cuisines and provide opportunities for students to experience diverse culinary traditions.

As part of USC's residential dining program, Parkside welcomes both meal plan holders and cash/credit card customers, providing a welcoming environment for the entire USC community.
