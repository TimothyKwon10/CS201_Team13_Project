Parkside Restaurant & Grill

  • Previous
  • Next
  • Previous
  • Next

Parkside Restaurant, located in the International Residential College at Parkside, serves international specialties from our four exhibition stations and also offers vegetarian & vegan selections, sandwiches, pizza and salads.

Enjoy monthly theme nights, where we transform the dining room, bring in entertainment and let our chefs “go all out” with the menu. See the event section for full details.

Each residential community is anchored by an all-you-care to eat dining venue. Specific meal plan participants enjoy unlimited access to all three dining halls. Each offers a different culinary experience appealing to a variety of dining preferences and nutritional lifestyles of our diverse student population.
